inroad INROAD
Wordmark only
✓ Do

Use the icon inside its rounded-square container. Keep clear space equal to icon width on all sides. Always use Lora serif lowercase for the wordmark.

✕ Don't

Don't recolour, stretch, or add effects to the mark. Don't use the bare mark below 24px without the container. Don't set the wordmark in sans-serif or title case.

07 — Copy voice
Principles
Direct, not pushy. Say what it does. No hype.
Confident, not arrogant. The product works — let it land quietly.
Student-level formality. Not a bank. Not a TikTok.
Short sentences. Under 20 words. One idea each.
Lowercase brand. Always inroad, never Inroad in body copy.
Example lines
"Stop applying into the void."
"Your next job starts with a conversation."
"Three matches. Every day."
Avoid: "leverage", "synergize", "game-changer", "revolutionary", "seamlessly"
