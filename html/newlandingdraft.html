How it works

From sign up to your first outreach
in minutes.

No complicated setup. Sign in with your email and you're ready to reach out.

Step 01

Sign in with your email

Your email verifies you instantly. No lengthy forms, no waiting, no credit card needed.

Step 02

Tell us your target role, industry and company size

We pull live job openings from job boards, career networks and trackers. Then we surface the people at those companies who could actually get you in.

Step 03

Review, approve and send.

We draft a personalised email tied to a real open role. You read it, edit it, and hit send.

Smart matching

Matched to real openings.

We don't just find contacts. We match you to people at companies with active roles that fit your profile. Alumni are prioritised because shared experience converts. When someone sees their university in your email, response rates jump.

  • ✓ Open roles pulled from job boards and career networks
  • ✓ Alumni from your university surfaced first
  • ✓ Name, position, company and contact info surfaced per match
